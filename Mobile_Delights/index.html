UNDER CONSTRUCTION: Mobile Delights

This page only makes sense if you check it out on your mobile.



display appropriate keyboard for input type="text"fields

Filling out a form on a mobile device can be a bit of a hassle if the form is not build properly: when typing in your email you want a keyboard with letters, '@' and '.', but you want numbers when typing in your phone number.
It is easy to build with inputmode. CSS-Tricks has a great article about it: Everything you ever wanted to know about inputmode.

Test it on your mobile:


inputmode=text (default)

inputmode=decimal

inputmode=email

inputmode=none

inputmode=numeric

inputmode=search

inputmode=tel

inputmode=url
